About the PS2 BIOS Archive

The PS2 BIOS Hub was established in 2024 with a singular mission: to provide the most technically accurate and accessible firmware resources for the PlayStation emulation community.

As original hardware inevitably fails due to capacitor leakage and disc drive degradation, the preservation of the system's firmware becomes critical. Our team meticulously verifies every BIOS version shared on this platform using MD5 and SHA1 checksums to ensure that users are installing clean, un-tampered software.

Our commitment to 2026 gaming standards means we are constantly updating our guides for the latest builds of PCSX2, AetherSX2, and specialized RetroArch cores.
